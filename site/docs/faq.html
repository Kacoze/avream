Frequently asked questions

Short answers for common Linux webcam setup questions.

How do I use Android phone as webcam on Linux?

Install AVream, launch GUI, enable service if prompted, scan phone, use selected phone, start camera, then pick AVream Camera in your app.

How do I use Android phone mic on Linux?

Start camera and choose AVream Mic in your meeting app.

Does AVream work with Zoom, Meet, and OBS?

Yes, if app supports standard Linux V4L2/Pulse-PipeWire devices.

USB or Wi-Fi?

USB is usually more stable, Wi-Fi is more convenient after setup.

Can AVream send PC audio to phone speaker?

Not in stable baseline without additional phone-side software.
